The Estate

A spectacular pastoral setting

Set on eight acres of picturesque grounds, this historic compound pairs a restored country manor home with a custom-built carriage house. Original architectural detail meets every modern convenience — a private oasis for relaxation, gathering, and quiet escape.

The Main House

A restored country manor

A large living room with a wood-burning fireplace opens to a sunroom overlooking the meadows. The library connects to a second screened sunroom above the lawn and garden, while a formal dining room with French doors leads to a private stone terrace for dining beneath the trees.

The chef's kitchen is outfitted with top-of-the-line appliances — Sub-Zero refrigeration, a Viking range, and two dishwashers — ready for everything from quiet mornings to full house gatherings.

  • 3 bedrooms · 3.5 baths
  • En-suite primary with attached private office
  • Two screened sunrooms · library
  • Formal dining with French doors to a stone terrace

Inside

Period charm, modern comfort

Throughout the manor, original architectural elements — fireplaces, millwork, and antique fixtures, including a beautiful antique commode — sit easily alongside contemporary comforts. Light pours through every room, framing views across the meadows and gardens.

The Carriage House

A custom-built guest house

The carriage house offers three bedrooms and two and a half baths, with an open living and dining space at its heart. French doors lead to a terrace overlooking the heated pool and the views of the vast land reserve beyond — ideal for guests, extended family, or a private retreat of its own.

  • 3 bedrooms · 2.5 baths
  • Open-plan living & dining
  • French doors to a poolside terrace
  • Outlook over the land reserve

The Grounds

Park-like, private, and serene

Eight acres of native trees, plantings, and flowering gardens surround the homes. A heated pool, broad stone terraces, and quiet lawns open onto a protected land reserve — endless room to wander, gather, and unwind.
